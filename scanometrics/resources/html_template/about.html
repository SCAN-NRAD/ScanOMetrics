About this software

SCAN-O-Metrics is a tool for automated calculation and visual representation of brain morphometry from T1-weighted MR datasets and comparison to normative ranges. It is based on the free software packages FreeSurfer and FSL and uses Python for statistical analysis and visualisation.

SCAN-O-Metrics was developed at the Support Center for Advanced Neuroimaging (SCAN), from Bern University's Institute for Diagnostic and Interventional Neuroradiology by

  • Christian Rummel (conception, statistics, programming, collection of normative data)
  • Roland Wiest (conception, collection of normative data)
  • Martin Zbinden (DICOM Server)
  • Yuliya Burren (quality control normative data)
  • Raimund Kottke, Stefan Schweizer, Rajeev K. Verma, Christian Weisstanner (collection of normative data)
