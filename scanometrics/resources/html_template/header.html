  • Morphometric results for the left hemisphere are shown on the left, those for the right on the right (cf. neurological convention). The same applies to surface maps. Note: In cross-sectional images, the left hemisphere is shown on the right (radiological convention).
  • Where appropriate, asymmetry indices between the hemispheres are shown as histograms.
  • Blue filled symbols are used for the patient (patient group), open symbols in magenta for the control group.
  • Statistical tests and linear or quadratic age trends are reported in relation to all controls. Controls matching the patient(s) in sex and MR sequence are shown as large open symbols, all other controls as small open symbols. Deviations at significance level p<0.01 (uncorrected/FDR) are highlighted in yellow/red.
  • For individual patients, the results are presented on the patient's own MRI, for patient groups on a standard brain.
